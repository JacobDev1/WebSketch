About


WebSketch has been built without any third-party libraries, for quick and easy sketching. No complicated layers, pencil brushes, or any extra functions. It has been built as a final year project for school, so it lacks any long term support.

If you have an inquiry, please forward it to my email.

Shortcuts

Draw: Left Click
Toggle Eraser: E
Change Brush Size: 1 - 5
Color Picker: P
Save: Ctrl + S
Load: Ctr + O
Clear Canvas: Ctrl + X
